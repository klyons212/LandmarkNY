Brooklyn Bridge,The Brooklyn Bridge is a hybrid cable-stayed/suspension bridge in New York City. It connects the boroughs of Manhattan and Brooklyn spanning the East River. The Brooklyn Bridge has a main span of 1595.5 feet (486.3 m) and a height of 276.5 ft (84.3 m) above mean high water. It is one of the oldest roadway bridges in the United States and was the world's first steel-wire suspension bridge.,bkbridge

Empire State Building,The Empire State Building is a 102-story Art Deco skyscraper in Midtown Manhattan. It was designed by Shreve and Lamb & Harmon and completed in 1931. The building has a roof height of 1250 feet (380 m) and stands a total of 1454 feet (443.2 m) tall including its antenna. Its name is derived from "Empire State" which is the nickname of New York. As of 2017 the building is the 5th-tallest completed skyscraper in the United States and the 28th-tallest in the world.,empirestate
